SPEC SHEET:

Process Option 1 – Section-by-Section Discussion

  1. Pick a section (e.g., Summary).

  2. Compare wording from each person’s spec sheet.

  3. Agree on best phrasing or merge elements.

  4. Move through each section in order.

Process Option 2 – Highlight & Focus

  1. Before the meeting, each person highlights their best sections/phrases.

  2. In the meeting, only discuss differences or gaps.

  3. Edit live in a shared doc.

REQUIREMENTS SHEET:

Process of Discussion:

  1. Everyone pastes their requirements lists into a shared document under each category (functional, non-functional, data, etc.).

  2. As a group:

    • Remove duplicates.

    • Merge similar items.

    • Comment on unclear or conflicting requirements.

  3. Flag any requirements needing policy/technical clarification for follow-up.

Expected outcome: A single, cleaned-up requirements list ready for refinement outside the meeting.
