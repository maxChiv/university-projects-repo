User Persona

  • What it is:
    A fictional representation of a real user type, based on research, interviews, or assumptions.

  • Purpose:
    Helps the team understand the user’s context, needs, goals, pain points, and background.

  • Format: Usually a profile card with:

    • Name

    • Role & demographics

    • Goals & motivations

    • Frustrations/pain points

    • Tools or workflows they use

    • USER, APPROVER, ADMIN - make it into 3 personas
