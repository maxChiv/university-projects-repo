Group Twenty Two Project : API EndPoint Checklist

Created by Mitchell Pooley on Sept 03, 2025

Before login:

  1. /

a.      NONE (Home page, links to sign up and login)

  1. /signup

a.      Users: PUT

  1. /login

a.      Users: GET

After login:

  1. /:userID/dashboard

a.      UserData: GET (Notification count, isAdmin)

b.      ProjectData: Get (Project overview details and ProjectID)

c.      ProjectData: PUT (Create a new project)

  1. /:userID/notifications

a.      UserData: GET (Notification message and ID)

  1. /:userID/notifications/:notificationID

a.      UserData: DELETE (Delete a notification)
