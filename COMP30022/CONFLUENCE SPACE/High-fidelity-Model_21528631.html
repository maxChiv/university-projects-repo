Group Twenty Two Project : High-fidelity Model

Created by Max Chivers, last modified on Sept 09, 2025

https://www.figma.com/team_invite/redeem/wqgiUIxvXuUe5AVoxyxN8B

The high-fidelity model was crafted with a clean and user-centric aesthetic inspired by Notion’s UX/UI, known for its minimalist layout, clear typography, and intuitive navigation. Additionally, Jay and I drew inspiration from established university cost and pricing tools. Specifically, ANU’s Costing, Pricing & Approval system and UWA’s Project Costing/Calculator templates. These tools guided our approach to balancing clarity, structured input flows, and professional tone.

  • The Notion influence is seen in our use of white space, modular panels, and simple yet elegant form styling.

  • From the ANU costing system, we adopted a straightforward layout with logical grouping of input fields and streamlined navigation through multi-step processes

  • The UWA tool contributed inspiration for clearly labeled sections, easy-to-follow instructions, and a clean form structure that accommodates complex data like personnel costs and indirect contributions

  • Additionally, currently used University of Melbourne web-tools are used as design inspiration for the project to keep it inline with similar design patterns.
