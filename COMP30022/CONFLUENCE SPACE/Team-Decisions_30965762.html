Group Twenty Two Project : Team Decisions

Created by Jay Schellens, last modified on Oct 10, 2025

Purpose of This Document:

This document records key decisions made by the team during the project. It ensures transparency, alignment, and provides a reference if decisions need to be revisited.


Our Decision-Making Process:

We make all major decisions during our in-person or online team meetings. These meetings occur at least once a week. We discuss the options carefully and then come to a decision based on majority vote. We take into consideration the opinions of the people that this decision affects the most.

3. Team Norms & Working Agreements

  • Meeting Schedule: at least weekly stand up, team meetings once per week, client meetings every two weeks, sprint planning every 2 weeks, sprint retrospective at the end of each sprint

  • Communication Expectations: respond promptly to any team discord messages

  • Coding Practices: use GitHub pull requests, follow coding standards

  • Definition of Done: code reviewed by at least one other person, fully tested, deployed to staging
